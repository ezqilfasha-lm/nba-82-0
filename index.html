What is 82-0?

82-0 is a free basketball draft game and roster builder. You spin a wheel to land on a real NBA team and season, draft one player from that squad, and choose where he plays. Fill all six spots, then simulate a full 82-game season to see how far your lineup goes — and whether it can do the impossible and win every night.

Built by fans, 82-0 mixes eras. Drop a 1996 Bull next to a 2016 Warrior next to a 2025 Thunder pick, and find out how your cross-era roster actually holds up across a full season, a playoff run and the award race.

Unlike fantasy basketball, 82-0 isn't tied to live games or the current week. It's a nostalgia-and-history game: pit legends against modern superstars, chase a perfect season, and settle once and for all whose roster is really the strongest.
