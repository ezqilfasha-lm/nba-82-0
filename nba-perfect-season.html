The Draft Wheel

Engine v8. Unit-based ratings (Offense / Playmaking / Rebounding / Defense) feed a point-margin match model where each game's result and score come from one seeded sample; the full 31-team league is simulated coherently and awards are contested league-wide. Draftable seasons span 1962 through the 2026 champions. Adapted for basketball from a Dixon-Coles football design. Curated iconic team-seasons with approximate stats; current-player overalls are informed by publicly listed NBA 2K ratings (2kratings.com) as a reference only. Backdrop teams and their marquee players carry simulated stat lines. Avatars are generated silhouettes — no player likenesses. Runs client-side, no backend.
Independent fan project. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the NBA or NBPA. Team and player names and statistics are factual references only; no logos, crests or likenesses are used.
